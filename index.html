About Us

Sur is Columbia University's premier Indian fusion a cappella group. Since the 2000s we've been bringing together our love for Indian and American music by making sick mash-ups of your favorite Bollywood and American pop songs! We're really excited about Sur's growth over the past few years, and hope you are too---come check us out when we're performing around campus!
